

TO-DO LIST

Technology used - HTML, CSS, javaScript, Jquery
Font and icon toolkit - Font Awesome 

This is a simple TO-DO List component developed in basic html, css, javascript and jQuery. 
Font-awesome used for the icons. you can toggle "To do"  using '+' sign at the top right corner of "To do"  list.
You can remove "To do" lists by clicking on trash button in the beginning of "To do" list.
